Who We Are

We are the Mechatronics Engineering class of 2025 at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology — a tight-knit batch of thirty engineers forging paths in robotics, automation, control systems, and beyond.

Our curriculum fuses mechanical, electrical, and computational thinking. From sensors to servos, from embedded systems to machine vision — we build things that move, sense, and think.

This space is ours: a living record of our journey through four years of engineering education.
